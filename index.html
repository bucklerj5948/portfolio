About

Hi, my name is rob and i am a graphic communications major at UW-Eau Claire. i am very passionate for all and anything that involves creativity. my biggest areas of inter- est are music, graphic design, photography//videography and fashion. some of my goals in life include being able to make music for a living, to work with big name music artists, to start my own apparel line, and to keep getting better and better at graphic design, photography, and videography. My work experience includes working and helping get a start-up media company up and running called Exist Media Co., started with a few other UW-Eau Claire students and I during 2020-21, which got me heavily into video work, as well as some photo work here and there, which I feel compliments graphic design really nicely!
